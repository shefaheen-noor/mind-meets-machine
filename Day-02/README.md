## 🧠 Types of Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is divided into different types based on its capabilities and level of intelligence.

The three major types of AI are:
1. Narrow AI
2. General AI
3. Super AI

---

## ⚡ 1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)

Narrow AI is designed to perform a specific task.

It can only work within limited functions and cannot think beyond its programming.

### 🌍 Real-World Examples
- ChatGPT
- Siri & Alexa
- Google Maps
- Instagram recommendations
- Face Unlock

#### ✨ Features
- Task-specific
- Fast and accurate
- Most common AI today

---

## 🌌 2. General AI (Strong AI)

General AI refers to machines that can think, learn, and perform tasks like humans.

This type of AI can:
- Understand emotions
- Solve problems independently
- Learn different skills

### ⚠️ Current Status
General AI does not fully exist yet.  
Scientists and researchers are still working on it.

---

## 🚀 3. Super AI

Super AI is a future concept where AI becomes more intelligent than humans.

It may:
- Think faster than humans
- Make advanced decisions
- Solve extremely complex problems

### ⚠️ Important
Super AI is still theoretical and has not been created.

---

## 📊 Comparison Table

| Type of AI | Capability | Exists Today? |
|---|---|---|
| Narrow AI | Specific tasks | ✅ Yes |
| General AI | Human-like thinking | ❌ No |
| Super AI | Beyond human intelligence | ❌ Future concept |
